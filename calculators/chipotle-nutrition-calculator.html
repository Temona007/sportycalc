Pro Tips for Healthier Chipotle Orders

  • Want more protein? Double your meat and skip the rice or go heavy on veggies.
  • Cutting calories? Skip cheese and sour cream, and load up on salsas and lettuce.
  • Post-workout meal? Add brown rice and extra protein for better recovery.
  • Watching sodium? Go light on cheese, salsas, and chips.
