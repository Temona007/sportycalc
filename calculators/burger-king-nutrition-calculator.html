How Our Burger King Calorie Calculator Works

We designed the calculator to be fast, intuitive, and accurate:

Browse the Menu

Choose from popular categories like Burgers, Chicken, Sides, Breakfast, Desserts, and Drinks.

Build Your Meal

Add any item to your cart. You can add the same item multiple times — the quantity will increase automatically.

Live Nutrition Totals

As you add or remove items, the calculator instantly updates:

  • Total Calories
  • Protein (g)
  • Carbs (g)
  • Fat (g)
  • Sodium (mg)

Daily Value Percentage

See what percentage your meal represents of a standard 2,000-calorie diet (the reference used on most nutrition labels).

Easy Reset

Clear your entire order with one click and start fresh.

The calculation logic is straightforward: each menu item has fixed nutritional values per serving. When you add quantity, we multiply the values and sum everything across your cart. Calories and sodium are shown as whole numbers, while protein, carbs, and fat are displayed to one decimal place for better accuracy.
