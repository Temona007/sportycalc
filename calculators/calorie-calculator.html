What is a Calorie Calculator?

A Calorie Calculator determines your personalized daily calorie needs by factoring in your Basal Metabolic Rate (BMR), activity level, and specific objectives. It builds on your Total Daily Energy Expenditure (TDEE) to suggest intake levels: maintain at your TDEE, create a deficit for fat loss, or a surplus for building mass. Calories are units of energy from food, and balancing them is key to health and fitness.

We use proven formulas like the Mifflin-St Jeor equation for BMR, combined with activity multipliers from sources like the Harris-Benedict principles, to deliver accurate estimates. This method is supported by nutritional research from bodies like the Academy of Nutrition and Dietetics.
