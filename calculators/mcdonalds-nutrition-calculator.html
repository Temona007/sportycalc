Pro Tips for Fitness-Friendly McDonald’s Meals

  • High-Protein Options: Grilled chicken sandwiches, Egg White Delight McMuffins, or premium burgers without bun.
  • Lower Calorie Meals: Salads + grilled protein + water instead of fries and soda.
  • Post-Workout: Add a shake or extra carbs if you’re using our Carbohydrate Calculator.
  • Sodium Awareness: Many items are high in sodium — balance with lower-sodium days.
  • Meal Hacking: Remove cheese, sauce, or bun to significantly cut calories and fat.
