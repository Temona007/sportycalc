About SportyCalc

Free, private, science-based fitness calculators. No sign-up, no tracking, no backend.

What We Do

SportyCalc provides BMI, BMR, TDEE, calorie, macro, body fat, heart rate zones, 1RM, protein, water, and calories-burned calculators. All run entirely in your browser.

Privacy First

Your inputs never leave your device. No accounts, no analytics that store health data. We believe fitness calculations should be private.

Science-Based

We use established formulas: Mifflin-St Jeor for BMR/TDEE, Navy method for body fat, Epley for 1RM, and standard MET values for activity calories.
