Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

1. Introduction

SportyCalc ("we," "our," or "us") operates sportycalc.com. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our fitness calculators and related services.

2. Information We Collect

Calculators: Our calculators (BMI, TDEE, BMR, etc.) run entirely in your browser. We do not collect, store, or transmit any data you enter. Your inputs never leave your device.

Contact form: If you submit a message through our contact form, we receive your name, email address, subject, and message. This information is used solely to respond to your inquiry.

Automatically collected data: We may use standard web analytics (e.g., traffic, referring sources) that do not personally identify you. Our hosting provider may log IP addresses for security and performance purposes.

3. How We Use Information

We use contact form submissions only to reply to your questions. We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties.

4. Cookies and Local Storage

We may use local storage (e.g., for theme preference or saved calculator results) on your device. This data stays on your device and is not sent to our servers.

5. Third-Party Services

Our contact form may be processed by a third-party service (e.g., Formspree). Please review their privacy policies for how they handle submitted data.

6. Data Security

We use reasonable measures to protect any data we receive. Calculator data is not transmitted, so no server-side storage of health or body metrics occurs.

7. Your Rights

You may request deletion of any contact form data we hold. Contact us at temona007@gmail.com.

8. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy. The "Last updated" date at the top indicates when changes were last made.

9. Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact us at temona007@gmail.com or through our contact form.
