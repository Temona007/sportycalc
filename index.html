Smart fitness, not stressful

At SportyCalc, we believe fitness should be smart, not stressful. That’s why we built a complete suite of free, accurate calculators designed specifically for athletes, military personnel, and anyone serious about their health and performance.

When you use our tools, you stop relying on guesswork and start making data-driven decisions.

With our TDEE Calculator, you’ll know exactly how many calories your body needs. Pair it with the Macro Calculator, Carbohydrate Calculator, and Fat Intake Calculator to build a nutrition plan that actually matches your goals — whether you’re cutting fat, building muscle, or maintaining peak performance.

Tracking body composition becomes effortless too. Our Body Fat Percentage Calculator, Army Body Fat Calculator, Waist-to-Hip Ratio, and Waist-to-Height Ratio tools give you clear insights into your progress. Military users especially love the Air Force PT Calculator, which follows the latest 2026 standards to help them stay mission-ready.

Want to know how old your body really is? Our Fitness Age Calculator reveals your biological fitness level based on science-backed VO2max estimation. Many users are shocked to discover their Fitness Age is 8–12 years younger — or older — than their actual age.

Training smarter is easier when you understand intensity. Use our Heart Rate Zone 2 Guide and Sleep Cycle Calculator to optimize recovery and endurance. Even occasional social choices become clearer with the Blood Alcohol Content (BAC) Calculator.

Whether you’re preparing for a military PT test, trying to lose stubborn fat, building muscle, or simply want to feel and perform better, our calculators give you the precise numbers you need to succeed.

No subscriptions. No ads in the tools. Just clean, fast, and accurate calculators that help real people get real results.
